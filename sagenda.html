Online Reservation System

  • Students must sign-up for a tutoring session on the website;
  • Students may reserve up to two 30-minute sessions per week;
  • Students may select one tutor and session time;
  • Students must provide their name, major, email address, and cellphone number to ensure proper communication with the Center

Restrictions

  • If a student is unable to make the session, the student must cancel the appointment at least twelve hours prior to the session scheduled so that other students may be assisted;
  • Failing to cancel appointments in advance will prohibit the student from reserving another session for one week;
  • More than three "no shows" (that is, no shows without cancelling) will result in the forfeiting of the student's rights to use the Writing Center for the entire semester

Additions

If all available sessions are booked, students are welcome to visit the writing center to see if any sessions are available.
